Advanced HGP

Advanced HGP is one of our innovative treatments for hair fall with impressive results and benefits of three different technology which is HGP, microneedling and low-level laser therapy all combined in one session. Treatment is quick, effective and virtually painless, thus giving far more efficient results than a regular HGP session.

HGP is a process that uses a patient's own cells to promote regrowth in the area of hair thinning or shedding on the scalp. Low-level laser therapy uses different wavelengths of light which has a unique ability to stimulates the epidermal cells which are responsible for the regeneration of hair follicles. It causes vasodilation and accelerates blood circulation around the hair follicle which helps to increase delivery of nutrients and oxygen to the hair follicles making the hair thicker, stronger and healthier. In Advance HGP we also combine Microneedling for better penetration of HGP and other hair growth factors. This triple technique combination procedure assures more efficient, faster and enhanced results.When Low-level Laser Therapy and Microneedling is used along with HGP, the positive effects of HGP treatment intensify. Though multiple sessions will be required, but one can expect long term results after 3-4 sessions of treatment. However, one can also take hair nutrients, multivitamin supplements prescribed by Dr.Kunal along with Advanced HGP for optimum results work on internal deficiency and hence maintain the results achieved from the treatment sessions.
